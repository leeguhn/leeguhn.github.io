IMMAI (Immersive AI)

IMMAI is a mobile game currently in development to make language learning immersive and engaging through AI/ML technologies. The team consists of 6 front & backend engineers and me as the artist and designer, contributing to the project when available. We are integrating interactive gameplay with adaptive learning mechanics to create a personalized experience for users.

As the Art & UI+UX Designer, my contributions include:

UI/UX Design: Crafting intuitive interfaces to ensure seamless navigation and accessibility.

Art Direction: Developing the visual style, including character designs, icons, and environments.

Collaboration with Programmers: Aligning UI/UX elements with AI-driven language-learning features to create a cohesive experience.

Asset Generation Strategy: Conceptualizing a system to dynamically generate assets as the game's scope expands.

I prioritize accessibility, engagement, and adaptability in my design process, ensuring that IMMAI remains both visually compelling and functionally effective for learners at various proficiency levels.
